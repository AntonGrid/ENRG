1. What’s Happening

The global energy system is the last bastion of total centralization. A handful of corporations decide what your electricity costs, who can sell it, and when to cut you off. The “green transition” has become a subsidy circus. Private producers (solar, wind, biogas) cannot fairly monetize their surplus.

$8 trillion — the size of the electricity market, and you have zero influence.

2. Our Solution: ENRG

ENRG is an operating system for energy freedom. It turns electricity production into mining of a liquid digital asset. Every token is backed by a real, verified kilowatt-hour.

Connect any source: solar, wind, hydro, biogas, hydrogen, or flare gas.

3. Proof-of-Production (PoP)

① Connect any generation source → ② IoT controller signs and sends proof every 10 minutes → ③ Smart contract verifies and mints ENRG tokens to your wallet. No wasted energy, only real value.

4. Energy Vault

Global liquidity pool. Raised funds buy real assets (solar farms, storage) and sign PPAs. Revenue is distributed via Buyback & Burn (deflation) and Staking (real yield).
