1. Problem

Centralized monopolies control the $8 trillion energy market. Private producers cannot sell surplus. Green subsidies enrich intermediaries, not generators.

2. Solution

ENRG turns electricity generation into mining of a liquid digital asset. Every token is backed by a verified kilowatt-hour.

3. Proof-of-Production

IoT devices sign and send proof of generation to the Solana blockchain. Smart contracts verify and mint ENRG tokens to the producer's wallet.

4. Energy Vault

Liquidity pool buys real energy assets. Revenue is used for Buyback & Burn, staking rewards, and reserve.

5. Tokenomics

Ticker: ENRG · Solana · 1B fixed supply · $0.01 initial · 60% community mining
