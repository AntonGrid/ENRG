1. Executive Summary

ENRG is a DePIN-native energy protocol that turns real-world energy production into a programmable, on-chain asset. Each SRC token represents 1 MWh of verifiable energy, backed by cryptographic proofs from IoT devices (ESP32, industrial Modbus/RS485 meters) and decentralized oracles.

2. The Problem – Centralized Energy Markets

Today’s energy markets are dominated by centralized utilities, opaque intermediaries, and fragmented data silos. Small producers cannot monetize surplus, consumers face opaque pricing, and renewable energy lacks transparent verification.

3. Our Solution – Proof-of-Production (PoP)

ENRG introduces Proof-of-Production: a cryptographic pipeline that connects physical energy devices to on-chain tokens.

Industrial Integration: Supports Modbus RTU/RS485 meters (Schneider, Siemens, ABB, Меркурий, Энергомера) via gateway firmware that translates readings into signed proofs.

Anti-Fraud System:

  • Hardware security: ATECC608 Secure Element for key storage and signing.
  • Replay protection: Monotonic nonce per device.
  • Physical limits: max_power_w per device, time-based limits (10 min).
  • Multi-oracle verification: Switchboard aggregates data from multiple oracles.
  • Anomaly detection: Monitoring sudden spikes, comparison with historical data.
  • Tamper detection: Enclosure sensors → key revocation.
