ENRG Technical Overview

Version 1.1 · April 2026

1. Architecture

IoT Devices → Oracles → Smart Contracts (Solana) → Energy Vault ↔ Users / Markets

2. Proof-of-Production

Every 10 min, IoT controller sends signed proof {device_id, timestamp, energy_kwh, signature}. Contract verifies signature, timestamp window, physical limit, then mints ENRG.

3. Smart Contracts (Solana / Rust)

  • registry – device registration and staking
  • mint_enrg – proof verification and minting
  • vault – revenue management
  • buyback_burn – automated market buy and burn
  • staking – holder yield distribution

4. API Endpoints

POST /api/v1/proof/submit – submit energy proof

GET /api/v1/device/{id}/status – device info

5. Energy Vault Economy

60% buyback & burn · 20% staking pool · 20% reserve

6. Roadmap

Genesis Q2–Q3 2026 · Mainnet Q4–Q1 2027 · Vault Q2–Q3 2027 · Expansion 2028+
